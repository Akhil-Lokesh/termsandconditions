Legacy G Suite editionsYou can no longer sign up for G Suite Basic, G Suite Business, or the legacy free edition of G Suite. If you have a G Suite edition, you can continue with your subscription and all related services. However, you can’t switch to a different G Suite edition.
Upgrade to Google Workspace
By upgrading to Workspace, you unlock 24/7 support and access to premium collaboration and security features, based on the edition you choose. You can upgrade to any of these Business editions:
Business Starter—Professional productivity suite with 30 GB pooled storage per user. Includes Gemini AI assistant in Gmail and access to Gemini app with with enterprise-grade data protections.
Business Standard—Enhanced productivity suite with 2 TB pooled storage per user. Includes Gemini AI assistant in Gmail, Docs, Meet, and more, and access to Gemini app with with enterprise-grade data protections.
Business Plus—Advanced productivity suite with 5 TB pooled storage per user. Includes Gemini AI assistant in Gmail, Docs, Meet, and more, and access to Gemini app with with enterprise-grade data protections.
To learn more and sign up, go to Business editions.
You can also upgrade to an Enterprise edition. For example, Enterprise Standard offers a complete suite of productivity and collaboration apps with flexible storage and enterprise-grade security and management. To switch to an Enterprise edition, contact Google Sales.
Compare G Suite and Workspace editions
Select your G Suite edition to compare key features with the Business editions and Enterprise Standard edition. For a full list of Business and Enterprise edition features, go to Compare Google Workspace editions.
